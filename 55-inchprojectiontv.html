55-Inch Projection Tv

Samsung released the supposed “world’s first” television. It was the 55-inch projection TV which launched in 1998. It became extremely popular which led to the development and many other flat screen tv which helped the Samsung company grow exponentially and allowed them to make even more products other than TVs. Later on they released the LCD TV ( released in the 2000s), the Bordeaux LCD Tv (released in 2006), The QLED TV (first time in the industry that a TV reached 100 percent color and was released around 2017),and then the Neo QLED (released in 2021)
